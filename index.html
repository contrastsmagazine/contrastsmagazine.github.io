Contrasts is accepting submissions from MIT graduate students, postdoctoral fellows, and affiliates for our inaugural magazine issue (“Portals”).

About the Magazine

Contrasts is an online magazine featuring public-facing works that can speak to a broad audience about the intersections of science, technology, and society. Through multiple genres—including creative nonfiction, visual essays, short films—it aims to foster critical reflection on STEM projects by highlighting the role of the humanities in making visible the politics and social contexts of science, technology, and medicine.
